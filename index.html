The Problem

Malawi has the talent.
It's missing the infrastructure.

Global freelance platforms exist. Malawian participation is near zero — not because of a skills gap, but because the infrastructure was never built for this market.

🔒

No trusted digital identity layer

Online transactions between strangers require verified identities. No platform has connected Malawi's National ID system (NRB/NRIS) to digital work — until now.

💱

USD-only payment rails lock people out

Fiverr and Upwork require USD accounts. Most Malawians don't have them. Airtel Money and TNM Mpamba are the real financial infrastructure here — and they've been ignored by every global platform.

📋

No path for first-time freelancers

Graduates with marketable skills have no portfolio, no credibility and no guidance on where to start. Existing platforms favour the already-experienced and shut out everyone else.

📱

Businesses hire through WhatsApp groups

SMEs source designers, developers and consultants through personal referrals and Facebook posts. It is slow, unverifiable, and inefficient — a broken market waiting to be fixed.

⚖️

No payment protection for project work

Freelancers get paid late or not at all. Clients receive incomplete work and have no recourse. Without escrow, neither side trusts the other enough to transact online.

🌍

Informal work is the only option

Skilled professionals are stuck in untracked, underpaid maganyu arrangements. The digital economy exists in Malawi — it just has no infrastructure to run on.

The Platform

Six infrastructure layers.
One platform.

Gig4Gig is not another marketplace. It is the foundational layer that makes digital work possible, trusted and accessible for any Malawian with a skill.

Digital Identity and Trust

National ID verification via NRB/NRIS, behavioural trust scoring, escrow payment protection and reputation history. Trust becomes measurable, not assumed.

How It Works

From signup to paid — in four steps

Every friction point that stops Malawians from earning online has been removed by design.

1

Verify your identity

Link your National ID via NRB/NRIS. One-time verification that unlocks platform trust permanently.

2

Build your profile with AI

Our AI assistant helps you create a professional service listing, set pricing and write your first proposal — in minutes.

3

Get hired. Deliver. Earn.

Clients hire you directly or respond to bids. Funds go into escrow. You deliver. Milestone met — funds released.

4

Get paid in MWK

Receive payment to Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba directly. No bank account needed. No USD. No waiting.
